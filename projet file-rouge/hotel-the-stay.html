THE STAY BOSPHORUS

Description

The Stay Bosphorus, a landmark mansion built in the 19th century by the Balyan family, the very same architects who designed many palaces and mansions in Istanbul. Located in the central neighborhood of Ortaköy, the melting pot of a variety of cultures in the past, The Stay Bosphorus boasts the city’s closest guest rooms to the Bosphorus. Surrounded by charming streets sprinkled with trendy restaurants as well as local cafes, jewelers and boutiques, the hotel is just a short trip away from the city’s major attractions and shopping malls, and allows for effortless travel by sea thanks to the enchanting ferry pier that rests right next to it.Situated on the crystal waters of the Bosphorus, neighboring the grand bridge that connects Europe to Asia. Needless to say, the rooms enjoy sweeping panoramic views of the Bosphorus from every angle, with the city and its ancient structures as its backdrop.Wake up at the Stay to the sights and sounds of the Bosphorus and see the Asian coast across.
